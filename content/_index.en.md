### Welcome to SwingConnection Leipzig!
We are a non-profit organisation of dance-loving people from Leipzig who are dedicated to the music and dances of the swing era. We offer ongoing dance classes for Lindy Hop, Balboa, Shag, Charleston, Blues and Solo Jazz at various levels, and organise a range of swing events.

Under the **[News]({{< ref "/aktuelles" >}} "News")** menu item you will find information about upcoming dates and news from our club.

If you are interested in taking a dance class with us, the **[Classes]({{< ref "/tanzkurse" >}} "Classes")** menu item has information about our course offerings.

We look forward to seeing you. See you soon on the dance floor!
