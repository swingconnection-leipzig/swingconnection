### New Course Round 2026
The next [**new course round**]({{< ref "/tanzkurse" >}}) starts on **16 March 2026**! Registration is **[open]({{< ref "/tanzkurse/#anmeldung" >}} "to registration")**!

### Minor Swing is back!
The [**Minor Swing**]({{< ref "/minorswing" >}} "Minor Swing") is returning! Starting **18 March 2026**, it will take place every Wednesday from 7:30 pm. Admission is free — we look forward to seeing you!

### We have moved
Since the beginning of January, the club premises are located at Goldschmidtstraße 16. Renovation and fit-out work is still ongoing. The doors are expected to open in mid-March.
If you are arriving by bike, please do not park on the path in front of the building, but rather in front of the Max Planck Institute.

### Spring Camp Mattstedt 2026
This year our beloved [**Spring Camp** at the Mühlenhof in Mattstedt]({{< ref "/mattstedt" >}} "Spring Camp at the Mühlenhof in Mattstedt") will take place again, this year from **04–07 June 2026**.
