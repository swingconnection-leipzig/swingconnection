Our traditional Spring Camp takes place this year from **04 June to 07 June 2026**. You can look forward to a wonderful weekend of relaxation, dance and swing music at the beautiful Mühlenhof Mattstedt in Thuringia. Expect three days of workshops covering Lindy Hop, Balboa, Shag, Blues and Solo Jazz. And of course you can let loose at the parties.

Get a feel for the relaxed atmosphere in Mattstedt here: [{{< icon class="fa-youtube" >}} YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=czMHDvKlnEA)

### Level
The camp is generally aimed at freshly-hooked swing dancers and those who want to work on their skills at a relaxed pace. For more advanced dancers there are opportunities to refresh skills and enjoy social dancing. The camp is ideal for people who have attended one or two beginner courses and want to dive into the world of workshops.

### Schedule & Programme
We ask for your course preferences when you register and then put together a mixed programme. There will be classes at various levels in Lindy Hop, Balboa, Shag, possibly Blues and Solo Jazz. Anyone who is interested in teaching a session is welcome to get in touch at mattstedt@swingconnection-leipzig.com. It doesn't have to be a strict swing dance session.

In general, the weekend is a time for shared dance exploration, exchange and relaxation.

Classes start on **Friday evening** with a get-to-know-each-other session. It is also possible to arrive on Thursday and enjoy the farm and the atmosphere.

If you are interested, there is the option to explore the area on a short walk along the Ilm (5–8 km).

In the evening there are cosy parties in the barn; on **Friday** the **Jivers** will be playing for us again. Afterwards the night can wind down to the sound of the Ilm river.

### Teachers
You will be taught by the instructors of SwingConnection Leipzig.

### Accommodation
The accommodation options have changed slightly. The dormitory is located in the barn on the island, and there are four nicely fitted-out mobile homes or living wagons. Additionally, two four-bed rooms in the main house are available. Alternatively, you can camp in your own tent on the meadow by the river.

For families, a room in the house is a good option. Please note that there are only a limited number of places available.

All beds in the dormitory have sheets. In the dormitory you will need a sleeping bag/blanket and a pillow.

Details about the accommodation options can be found during registration.

### Catering
We take care of your culinary needs. Full board is provided with breakfast, coffee & cake and an evening meal. The food is exceptionally good! Vegan and gluten-free food unfortunately cannot be guaranteed. Vegetarians will be very happy, as we will again have meat-free dishes. Coffee, tea and water are included in the catering price. Other drinks can be purchased at moderate prices.

### Location
Mattstedt is a small village in Thuringia right on the Ilm river, roughly in the middle between Weimar, Jena and Naumburg. The large Mühlenhof farm lies idyllically in the greenery. Here we can dance, celebrate, run our workshops and welcome early summer.

https://www.muehlenhofmattstedt.de/muehlenhof-gallery

### Getting There
By **car** via the A9 (Naumburg) or A4 (Erfurt/Weimar), always heading towards Apolda.

For travel by **train**, the same applies: via Erfurt/Weimar or Naumburg to Apolda.

**Carpooling** has proven worthwhile. About two weeks before the camp we will provide a document for coordination.

The address is: **Mühlenhof Mattstedt, Unterdorf 115, 99510 Mattstedt**.

Travel is at your own responsibility.

Those who have registered for Thu–Mon can arrive on Thursday from 5:00 pm. Those arriving on Friday are welcome in time for coffee.

### Costs
The costs for the workshop and accommodation can be found in the registration portal.

We have a contingent of 5 places for which we do not charge fixed workshop fees, to support people who find it difficult to afford participation.

### Notes
- Participation in our events requires acknowledgment and adherence to our **[Code of Conduct](../Code_of_Conduct_SCL.pdf)**.
- For overnight stays in rooms, please bring your own bed linen.
- For overnight stays in the dormitory, please bring a sleeping bag and/or blanket and pillow.
- Earplugs, a sleep mask and a torch can be helpful in the dormitory.
- If the weather is cold that weekend, warm clothing is important as there is no heating in the dormitory!
- Please also bring your own towels and bath towels.
- The following items can also be useful: flip-flops, power strip, charging cables, water bottle, thermos cup, standing mirror, walking shoes.
- If body-related classes are on offer (e.g. yoga), it is worth bringing a yoga mat or camping mat.
- Please be aware that mobile phone reception in Mattstedt is poor.
- In recent years, ticks and mosquitoes on the meadow and in the forest have been quite active. Bring insect repellent and tick protection if needed.
- Don't forget your dance shoes ;-)
- If you have food intolerances, please ask the kitchen team on site which dishes you should avoid.
- In our experience, the person doing check-in has limited capacity during arrival times. If it is your first time in Mattstedt, ask people who have been before to show you around — they will be happy to help.
- Some cash (approx. €20) will be needed for the band, and drinks in the evening are billed in cash upon departure. There are no ATMs in the village.
- Further information about the schedule and travel will be put together for you before the event.
- If you have any further questions, get in touch at mattstedt@swingconnection-leipzig.com
