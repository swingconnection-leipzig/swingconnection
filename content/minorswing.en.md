Finally putting all that hard-earned dance material to the test, dancing freely, chatting, or simply watching and marvelling. **Every Wednesday from 7:30 pm** the Minor Swing takes place. The Minor Swing is our weekly social dance for Lindy Hop, Balboa, Charleston and Shag. The DJs of the SwingConnection provide a varied mix of music. From authentic swing of the 1930s to neo-swing of today, everything is there. Admission is free. Beginners and interested people are always welcome.

### Time
Wednesdays from 7:30 pm to 10:30 pm.
