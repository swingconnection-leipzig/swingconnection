Have you caught the swing bug in our beginner courses and want more? We are always happy to welcome new members. As a member of SwingConnection Leipzig you can actively participate in club life. You can also attend all regular dance classes free of charge throughout the year and receive discounted access to other events organised by the SCL. If you have any questions about membership, feel free to speak to us at our events or send us an [email](info@swingconnection-leipzig.com).

#### Annual Fee
The current annual membership fee is €230 or €194 (reduced). The reduced rate applies to holders of a school ID, student ID, Leipzig Pass or disability pass. In addition, a one-time registration fee of €30 is due.

#### Requirements
The formal requirement for membership is prior participation in at least two courses. You can join the club from the 1st of any month and the application must reach the board 10 days before the start of the month. We also expect new members to actively participate in club life. This includes a willingness to take on tasks and to register on our [club communication platform on Wechange](https://wechange.de/group/swing-connection-leipzig/).

#### Apply for Membership
Membership can be applied for through our registration portal.
