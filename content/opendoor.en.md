Would you like to try swing dancing, but aren't sure which of our courses to start with? Do you want to see whether swing dancing could be for you? Or do you simply feel like trying something new, getting moving and getting to know a dance-enthusiastic club? Come to our Open Doors Day!

On **Sunday, 19 January 2025**, taster classes for various styles of swing dance awaited you. Afterwards you could take your newly learned steps straight to the floor at our **[Tea Dance]({{< ref "/swingteatime" >}} "Tea Dance")**. Throughout the day there was coffee and cake and the opportunity to get to know the club and our dance courses.

As with our regular classes, you could participate in the taster classes without a dance partner. We rotate partners with every exercise or song. (Of course you can also come as a couple and not switch if you prefer!) If you are not yet sure whether you would prefer to lead or follow, you can try both roles in the class.

---

### Taster Classes

{{< admonition success "2:00–3:00 pm Lindy Hop" >}}
Lindy Hop, which originated in the 1930s in African American ballrooms in Harlem, is today probably the best-known swing dance and is danced (almost) everywhere in the world. Lindy Hop can be danced relaxed to slower music or dynamically to faster swing music and is particularly varied and creative.
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "3:00–4:00 pm Lindy Hop Switch (with prior experience)" >}}
Do you already have experience in Lindy Hop and want new inspiration for your dancing? In this class you learn how to fluidly switch between the roles of leader and follower. Discover the freedom and fun of switch dancing — for more creativity and flexibility on the dance floor!

*The class takes place in the Erker.*
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "3:00–4:00 pm Collegiate Shag" >}}
Collegiate Shag (or simply Shag) is an athletic swing dance to fast music that will definitely make you break a sweat. Shag is full of energetic and playful moves, creative footwork and plenty of room for improvisation.
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "4:00–5:00 pm Balboa" >}}
Balboa is a swing dance that originated in the 1920s/30s on crowded dance floors in Southern California. Accordingly, it is danced in a close and compact embrace. Balboa is a mix of creative footwork and quick turns, and is especially great for gliding smoothly across the dance floor even to fast music.

*The class takes place in the Erker.*
{{< /admonition >}}
