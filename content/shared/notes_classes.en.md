Participation in our events requires acknowledgment and adherence to our **[Code of Conduct](../Code_of_Conduct_SCL.pdf)**.
In the courses, dance partners are regularly rotated. If you do not want to switch, you don't have to.
You can flexibly choose whether to practise as a follower or a leader in the course. You can also stick to just one role.
Instructors are encouraged to use gender-neutral language. We generally speak of "leader" and "follower" (neutral terms), and each person can decide which role they want to dance.
Classes are held in German, but can be given in English if needed.
Please arrive on time and plan for time to get changed and swap your shoes.
We recommend comfortable, flat, clean trainers or dance shoes. High heels are not suitable.
