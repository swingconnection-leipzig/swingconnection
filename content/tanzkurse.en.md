### Level Descriptions
- Basics: This course is suitable for everyone with no prior experience.
- Mixed: This course is suitable for everyone who already has some experience, e.g. Basics courses at the SCL.
- Intermediate (Int): This course is suitable for those who are already more advanced, e.g. have attended several Mixed courses and have a solid grasp of the basics.

{{< admonition success "Balboa Mixed (Mon 6:30 pm)" false >}}
**Refresh your Basics**

This Balboa Mixed course is aimed at everyone who already has some basic knowledge of Balboa, for example from a Basics course, but wants to revisit, repeat and consolidate all the fundamentals before building on them.
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "Balboa Mixed/Intermediate (Tue 6:30 pm)" false >}}
**Quick and Slow - the two ends of Balboa**

In this course we look together at how to dance the fundamental Balboa figures in a relaxed way at all tempos, with a focus on improvisation and joy in the dance connection. Gentle and connected dancing is the priority. If you feel like embracing and exploring that, you're in the right place.
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "Balboa Intermediate (Wed 7:15 pm)" false >}}
**Shine your Balboa**

Do you feel at home in Balboa? Do moves come easily to you and do you enjoy dancing together? Then this course is exactly right for you! We pick up where your skills already shine — and bring even more depth. Together we dive into the finer details, explore shimmering variations in footwork and refine complex figures.
If you feel like falling in love with details and working towards making even demanding sequences feel smooth, connected and musical — then you've found your place.
{{< /admonition >}}

[//]: # ({{< admonition success "Lindy Hop Basics &#40;Tue 7:45 pm&#41;" false >}})
[//]: # (Lindy Hop is the best-known swing dance and has its roots in African-American communities in the 1920s–40s. It is mostly danced in open position and has a very joyful and uplifting feel. Like jazz and swing music, the dance thrives on improvisation, exchange and togetherness. We learn simple elements from Swing and Solo Jazz to be able to dance in a varied and musical way, and place great emphasis on equality, consent and pleasant connections. "Switch" means that both dance roles &#40;following and leading&#41; can be tried. Those who only want to lead or only follow are welcome to do so. We use gender-neutral language and aim to create a queer-friendly and open atmosphere. In the course we also provide historical and cultural context and show videos of artists from this context, to collectively develop a feel for the origins, traditions and significance of the dance.)

{{< admonition success "Lindy Hop Intermediate (Wed 6:30 pm)" false >}}
**Mixed Moves**

This course is aimed at those who are grounded in their basics and regularly attend social dances. Together we want to explore moves and musicality. Prerequisites: Tuck Turn, Swing Out, confident 6-count place change, basic principles of leading and following, confident switching between 8-count and 6-count dynamics.

{{< admonition success "Shag Mixed (Wed 6:00 pm)" false >}}
If high BPM also makes your heart race, Collegiate Shag is exactly right for you! We want to continue teaching fun moves and footwork from this cheerful and energetic swing dance while having lots of fun with you! You should have a solid grasp of the Collegiate Shag basic step and can build on what you have already learned in this course. Everyone can lead, follow or switch roles.
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "Shim Shag (Mon 6:30 pm)" false >}}
Here comes the fast-paced Collegiate Shag variation of the classic Shim Sham. An energetic solo routine danced to faster music that is a real blast with its bouncy footwork. You will learn the complete choreography step by step and get a feel for the characteristic Shag bounce. Ideal for people with basic Shag knowledge and beginners who are in the mood for pace and a good dose of fun. After the class, participants can stay on a bit longer to practise.

**Course language:** German/English

**Dates:** 30.03, 13.04, 20.04, 27.04

*Can be booked with or without the Shim Sham course.*
{{< /admonition >}}

{{< admonition success "Shim Sham (Mon 6:30 pm)" false >}}
The Shim Sham is probably the most well-known solo jazz routine in the swing dance scene — a communal "dance anthem" danced worldwide at socials, festivals and shows. Whether refreshing or as a newcomer — in just 2 sessions you will learn the complete routine step by step and at the same time get a feel for the typical energy and playfulness of Solo Jazz. After the class, participants can stay on a bit longer to practise.

**Course language:** German/English

**Dates:** 16.03, 23.03

*Can be booked with or without the Shim Shag course.*
{{< /admonition >}}
