Dance classes alone aren't enough for everyone. We swing dancers want more! After the work comes the play — in our case events for free dancing, camps and workshops! Here you will find an overview of what SwingConnection organises outside of regular classes.

We announce our events in advance under [News]({{< ref "/aktuelles" >}} "News").

### Minor Swing
Finally putting all that hard-earned material to use, dancing freely, chatting, or simply watching and marvelling. The Minor Swing is our weekly social dance for Lindy Hop, Balboa, Charleston and Shag. The DJs of the SwingConnection provide a varied mix of music. From authentic swing of the 1930s to neo-swing of today, everything is there. Admission is free. Beginners and interested people are always welcome. More information can be found on the [Minor Swing]({{< ref "/minorswing" >}} "Minor Swing") page.

### Swing Tea Time
We regularly invite you to the Swing Tea Time. Our dance studio is filled with the scent of freshly baked cakes and the sounds of Louis Armstrong, Django Reinhardt or Ella Fitzgerald. Admission is free. Beginners and interested people are always welcome. More information can be found on the [Swing Tea Time]({{< ref "/swingteatime" >}} "Swing Tea Time") page.

### Friday Night Balboa
We regularly host Friday Night Balboa. More information can be found on the [Friday Night Balboa]({{< ref "/fridaynightbalboa" >}} "Friday Night Balboa") page.

### Spring Camp Mattstedt
The highlights of the year are our camps every spring and summer. Out into nature and depending on your preference, relax or dance the days away. The Spring Camp at the Mühlenhof in Mattstedt is aimed at both newly-hooked dance beginners and people who have been dancing longer. Here you spend a whole weekend with nothing but swing in your head and get to know your fellow course participants and other instructors better. More information can be found on the [Mattstedt]({{< ref "/mattstedt" >}} "Mattstedt") page.

### Swing Brunch
We regularly host a Swing Brunch.

### Workshops
On individual weekends we occasionally offer smaller and larger workshops. These include, for example, learning a swing routine, consolidating basics or learning new moves. We also offer weekend intensive courses for beginners.

### Open Doors Day
Once or twice a year we host an Open Doors Day, at which we present our club and offer taster sessions for the various dances that can be learnt with us.

### Performances and Taster Classes for Private Events
We also offer performances and taster classes for private events such as weddings, birthday parties and corporate events. Feel free to send us an email at info@swingconnection-leipzig.com.

### Swing Calendar for Leipzig
In the club-independent calendar [**"Swing in Leipzig"**](https://kalender.digital/0c529f4b4448ea55b992) you will find an overview of *all* swing events in Leipzig (classes, workshops, dance evenings, social dances, concerts).
